Staff Help

Managing staff is divided up into two parts: Employees and Departments.

Employees

Employees are managed from the employee list, where you can click to edit or delete some employee. Each employee has basic information such as name, title and contact information, but employees also belong to departments.

Departments

A department is a named group of employees where each member can be ordered and have another title, instead of the default employee title, assigned to him or her. This allows for roles of employees to change when presented in a department.
