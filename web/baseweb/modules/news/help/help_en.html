News Help

News are small to medium text entries that you can use on your website to publish information or changes in your organization. News items are available as news-list, with the most recent entries, or as an archive where older entries can be viewed.

To create a new news item, click on the "Create new" button and fill out the form. It's important that you mark the entry with your signature if you share the work of creating news items for your website.

In the list of news items you can click to edit or delete news items.
